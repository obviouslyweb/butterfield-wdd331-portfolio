Personality

I've said that if I wasn't majoring in Computer Science, I'd probably be in Psychology because I find personality systems and human interaction dynamics fascinating. About me, I'm naturally introverted, passionate in the things I do, and want to leave this world better than it was when I entered it. On the downside though, I'm not the best with criticism, I'm a HEAVY perfectionist in everything I do, and I can burn myself out easily. I'm also pretty sure I'm neurodivergent too, although I don't have any sort of "official" diagnosis.

You can probably guess a lot about me from this website I've made. That's, uh, kind of why this website exists, so I can get out of my shell somewhat. So if you've made it this far, uh... thanks!

Communication

In terms of internet activity, I tend to keep to myself and don't post much as I don't want to let my opinions get in the way of my passion and work. There's a few exceptions to that of course, but it's rare, usually when I'm REALLY passionate about something that I feel needs to be heard.

I am pretty bad with communication when it comes to people I don't yet know; I'm trying to work on that, but I apologize for any awkwardness or if you have to keep pinging me. Discord is the best for contact IMO since I can remind myself to respond, but you could also try other platforms too, I guess...

Workflow & Project Timelines

In terms of when new projects are released, I have no schedule of any kind; my mantra is "new projects will come out when they're ready." This is something I've had to adapt to in order to deal with perfectionism (and college).

Why This Site Exists
