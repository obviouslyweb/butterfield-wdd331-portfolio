Join me and wander into the Dark Ride Zone.

The Dark Ride Zone is a theme-park focused variety channel created by TheWebCon, featuring two types of videos:

  • The Dark Ride Zone, tightly-edited video essays about theme parks that may be hiding a few secrets here and there...
  • The Overflow Zone, looser commentary-style videos where I talk mostly unscripted about things going on in the world of the theme park industry

As The Dark Ride Zone is a very unique channel, its content requires a lot of additional work, which means videos take a LONG time to come out. But I am really passionate about the project still, and want to keep making more videos for it, so if you like what you see, don't hesitate to subscribe!

Song Visualizers

I don't know about you, but when I'm listening to music on YouTube, having a visualizer of some kind is REALLY cool. I also make music, so I have the control to do just that for my own music projects. As a result, I've made a lot of different visualizers for my songs and like to really test my editing skills and see how cool looking I can make them.

For example, the video in this card (a visualizer for an original song titled forgotten system settings) was hand-edited by yours truly. After making the album cover art, I exported all the assets from that and recreated it, then animated it with keyframes to simulate a real interface. Did it take forever and also nearly kill my sanity? Let me answer that question with a question: isn't it cool looking?

Song visualizers are posted to my main channel, TheWebCon, when music is released.
