without music, life would be a mistake

Music is the spice of life, the thing that helps me get work done that I don't want to do as well as find meaning. To give back, I've started making my own music to give back to the artists who inspired me.

You can find my music on the following platforms:

YouTube

Bandcamp

SoundCloud

Spotify

MuseScore

Please note that not all tracks may be present on certain platforms due to licensing restrictions, such as game covers or remixes.

For a sampling of my works, take a listen to the tracks below...

forgotten system settings

Somewhere in the world, a game console plays this track on loop indefinitely. It's ready for someone to pick up the controller, to move the cursor, to edit these settings that have been burning into the TV for hours... and yet, no one comes. And so the song plays, and plays, and plays...

Inspired by ambient music from game consoles that we all grew up with, specifically Nintendo's Wii U and Nintendo 3DS system music. Just like this work-in-progress track!

We've Come So Far (Promising Tomorrows) - Orchestral Cover

Space Mountain is an iconic and popular roller-coaster at Disney theme parks across the world, but what you may not know is that it had a beautiful and triumphant main theme! Here's my full orchestrated cover of that track.

A song that shows my love for theme parks, orchestral music, and one that I'm still quite proud of to this day.

BIG SHOT - Cover

Late to the party by about 4 years, here's my cover of BIG SHOT from DELTARUNE Chapter 2.

A recent cover of a wild song from one of my favorite games.

The spectacular album art of Spamton NEO was made by DrGrey! Hats off to him for his amazing work.

Gadget Room - Chiptune Remix

Who knew a licensed kids game would have music this good?!

Gadget Room from Club Penguin: Elite Penguin Force [Nintendo DS], remixed in Chiptune by yours truly. This one's pretty old now, around 5 years or so, but I still think it holds up as one of my best chiptune remixes/covers/arrangements/etc.

Requiem of the Corrupt

Tonight, we mourn the loss of those who have not yet passed.

A solemn then intense piano tune about the tragedy of someone; or, rather, something. A track written for a friend's project back in 2021. While not the fanciest song, it's one I still have a soft spot for.
